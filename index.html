Words Project (7 Days)

Read a good book for 7 days and collect 50 words. Write their meaning and use them in sentences. Try to solve a problem by using these words in a story.

Exercise: Type the meaning of "Innovation"

Sentence Structure Project

Learn how to construct sentences and deconstruct them. Rebuild sentences to solve a problem, e.g., giving clear instructions.

Exercise: Rearrange to form a correct sentence: "is / my / project / interesting"

Oral English Project

Learn sounds and transcription. Solve a problem by explaining your daily routine aloud and transcribing it.

Exercise: Type transcription of: "I wake up at 7 AM and eat breakfast"

Composition Project

Write letters or essays on an important topic. Solve a real problem by your writing, e.g., "How to keep your city clean".

Comprehension Project

Read passages and answer questions. Solve a problem based on what you read, e.g., summarize and suggest a solution.

Passage: "The city has too much plastic waste. Citizens need to reduce, reuse, and recycle."

Question: What is one solution to reduce plastic waste?
